## Context
The app should feel like a sharp operator's desk: calm, premium, and practical rather than chatty.

## Decisions
- Use a single-page dashboard with intake on the left and structured output on the right.
- Make AI central to extracting deliverables, assumptions, risks, scope tiers, and the reply draft.
- Emphasize clarity, urgency, and business confidence over novelty gimmicks.
- Optimize for "paste the messy ask -> review scope options -> send the reply".
